Lands of Divine Rulers

the higher energies reside in different planes...

JUDGEMENT DOMAIN
Our almighty judge rules the territory that produces most of our energy. All thanks to the Mechanisms' hard work the Higher Energies keep running. Human wrongdoers with administrative charges also reside here, dutifully serving their sentence for the betterment of society.
JUSTICE DOMAIN
Michael, the angel of Justice, mostly lets his robots govern themselves and focuses on delivering fiery justice upon wrongdoers, so everything is very self-sufficient and progressive. The few humans that live there are the smartest of their kind, allowed to a secluded part of the Ultimate Knowledge Library.
WHEEL OF FORTUNE DOMAIN
Since Rota, the Wheel of Fortune angel, does not produce any minions, its territory is mostly inhabited by humans. They are governed by Rota’s system of randomly generated outputs that determine everything: from the number of rations per family to marriage and child production. Highly efficient!
DEATH DOMAIN
The territory of Curio, the Death angel.
Mostly consists of piles of waste that the angel resides upon and constantly remakes into new objects or Materials. Due to pools of mud and wet clay everywhere, the area is unsafe for most other creatures, although thanks to Curio’s friendship with the Devil angel, lots of ceramic Toys are produced there.
DEVIL DOMAIN
The territory of the Devil Angel, commonly (and unfairly) dubbed 'Hell' by many. The place, where everything is meant to cater to one's every desire, administered by the kindest and most compassionate ruler of all. Shaped like a dollhouse, the place will surely fill any human with peaceful nostalgia and any creature with tender childlike joy! For that very reason, many mentally diseased are sent here for reformation...and many stay for utmost care!
