I ADORE TOYS!

Who doesn't?

All toys are handmade by the Devil Angel, so each and every one is unique and has features that reflect the state the angel was in when making them.
As they are governed by the most loving and caring creature in the universe, they have the best living conditions - their own little rooms with their own little furniture - and all they have to do in return is assist and entertain their master. Toys can also serve as companions to humans held at the Devil Domain. Toys are powered by magical energies, therefore don’t need food or any other kind of nourishment. They are, however, inherently social creatures and need constant affection and love to be fulfilled.
An abandoned toy will feel unmotivated and lonely, which can lead to great mental breakdowns, so stock up on hugs!
Dolls are the most common Toy type, made with the Devil Angel's porcelain likeness in mind. Dukes and Duchesses for tea parties, nurses for the hospitals, maids for private quarters - they are organized into a strict hierarchy of nobility and working class, with the lowest ranking dolls responsible for tying shoelaces.
But there are also plush animals, paper puppets, wooden soldiers, music box figurines and even teeny tiny sugar angels. All of them work hard every day to create a comfy and cozy atmosphere for the humans. The Devil Domain and its citizens are responsible for textile production, entertainment and medicine.
